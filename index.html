Frequently Asked Questions

How does In-a-Flash Delivery work?

We match senders with couriers instantly based on preferences and pricing.

Can I choose my delivery price, or does it get assigned automatically?

You can choose! Couriers offer pricing, and you select the best fit.

How do I know my package is safe?

All couriers are verified, tracked in real-time, and reviewed by users.

What happens if no courier picks up my request?

You can resubmit or adjust your price/time to increase interest.

How do I contact my courier?

Through in-app chat or phone once your delivery is accepted.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit, debit, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal.
